# Wethlete Ad System

## Overview
This system defines how every creative is built, tested, and scaled.

Every ad follows:
Use Case → Angle → Bucket → Format → Awareness Stage

No exceptions.

---

## Core Rule

Every creative must answer:

Who is this for  
What moment are we capturing  
What emotion is driving this  
What outcome do they want  

If this is unclear, the ad is wrong.

---

## Step 1: Use Case

A use case is:

WHO + WHERE + WHEN + WHAT IS HAPPENING

Examples:

Nurse at end of 12h shift with swollen legs  
Traveler during long-haul flight with heavy ankles  
Athlete after training with inflamed legs  
Standing worker finishing long day in pain  

Use case = the moment we enter

---

## Step 2: Angle (Emotional Driver)

Angle = emotion inside the moment

Structure:

Human Truth → Sub Truth → Angle

Examples:

Human Truth: I sacrifice my body for others  
Sub Truth: Standing 12 hours destroys my legs  
Angle: Your legs deserve support too  

Human Truth: I want to show up at my best  
Sub Truth: I land flights feeling destroyed  
Angle: Arrive like you didn’t just fly 14 hours  

Rule:

Never start from product  
Always start from emotion

---

## Step 3: Content Buckets

### Video (60–70%)

Use for:
Emotion, storytelling, demos

Best for:
Transformation, lifestyle, explanation

---

### Static (30–40%)

Use for:
Fast testing, bold claims

Best for:
Hooks, single benefits, clarity

---

### UGC

Use for:
Trust, relatability

Best for:
Testimonials, real experiences

---

### AIVO

Use for:
Testing copy fast

Best for:
Angle validation without reshooting

---

## Step 4: Frameworks

Every ad must use one:

### Problem → Solution

---

### Split-Screen

Without vs with

---

### Us vs Them

---

### Testimonial

Real people  
Real results  
Proof

1 angle  
1 awareness stage  
1 format

problem → emotion → outcome
